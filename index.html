CradleAI

Baby keeping you up at night?

For new parents, a good night's sleep is an unattainable luxury. Constant interruptions leading to sleep deprivation is not healthy for parents. Babies left alone to 'cry it out' results in unneccessary stress and may even lead to developmental issues later in life.

Enter CradleAI - a smart cradle designed to help parents and their babies sleep better. Our proposed solution uses motion sensors to detect baby waking up and employs a cutting edge artificial intelligence to detect the baby's mood. CradleAI dynamically generates a lullaby and controls the rocking motion of the cradle - attempting to put the baby back to sleep without requiring parental supervision unless absolutely neccessary.
